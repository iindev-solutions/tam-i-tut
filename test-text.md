🚀 What is TamITut?
TamITut solves the "liquidity and trust" crisis for expats living in Da Nang, Vietnam. Finding reliable housing, transport, or services often involves navigating scattered Facebook groups, unverified Telegram chats, and high risks of scams.
We provide a single, trusted entry point:

    Verified Listings: Housing, bikes, jobs, and local services vetted by moderators.
    Community Safety: Public blacklist of scammers and evidence-based reporting.
    Seamless Access: Instant access via Telegram Mini App (TMA) with a responsive Web fallback for SEO and desktop users.

💡 Core Philosophy

    Trust > Volume: We prioritize verified providers over sheer quantity.
    Speed > Complexity: Search-driven UX. No deep category trees.
    Safety First: Built-in reputation system and moderation tools.
